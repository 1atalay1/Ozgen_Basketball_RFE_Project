Atahan TURGUT

Atahan TURGUT was born in 2003. He started playing basketball when he was 9.

He has played for some big Turkish clubs such as Fenerbahçe Beko, Karşıyaka, Mavibirlik and Söğütsen Seramik.

He is 2 time Scoring leader in Parkur, Champion of 2021-2022 U21 Tournament and ABEL.

He has been doing coaching for 5 years

Tolga ÖZGEN

Tolga Özgen was born in 2002. He started playing basketball when he was 8.

He has played for some big Turkish clubs such as Beşiktaş,Anadolu Efes and Söğütsen Seramik.

He is 4 time assist leader in Parkur and MVP of 2021 tournament, Champion of 2021-2022 U21 Tournament and ABEL.

He has been doing coaching for 3 years

Osman Gazi ATALAY

Osman Gazi ATALAY was born in 2003. He started playing basketball when he was 10.

He has played for some big Turkish clubs such as Beşiktaş,Mavibirlik and Söğütsen Seramik.

He is the MVP of U16 tournament and considered as the best basketball player in Parkur, Champion of 2021-2022 U21 Tournament and ABEL.

He has been doing coaching for 2 years
